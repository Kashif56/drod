Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our piano and organ subscription

Are the lessons suitable for beginners?

Yes, our lessons are designed for all skill levels, including complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to more advanced techniques. Our personalized approach ensures that you learn at your own pace.

Is it possible to learn without a musical instrument?

While having access to a piano or keyboard is recommended for practice, we offer initial lessons that focus on music theory and reading notes that can be completed without an instrument. However, to get the full benefit of the program, you'll eventually need access to a piano, keyboard, or organ.

What happens if I have a question during the lesson?

You can send your questions directly to our teachers through our WhatsApp support system. Our teachers are available to answer your questions and provide guidance throughout your learning journey.

Can I freeze my subscription?

Yes, you can freeze your subscription for up to 30 days per year. This is perfect for vacations or busy periods when you might not have time to practice. Simply contact our support team to arrange the freeze period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and direct bank transfers. All payments are secure and processed through industry-standard encryption.

How long does it take to see results?

Most students start to see noticeable progress within 4-6 weeks of consistent practice. However, everyone learns at their own pace, and our personalized approach ensures that you'll make steady progress regardless of your starting point.

Do you need to know how to read characters?

No, we teach music notation from the ground up. You don't need any prior knowledge of reading music. Our step-by-step approach makes learning to read music accessible to everyone.

How long should you practice per day?

We recommend 20-30 minutes of practice daily for beginners. Consistency is more important than duration. Regular, focused practice sessions yield better results than occasional long sessions.

Are the lessons also suitable for children?

Yes, our program includes specialized content for children aged 6 and up. The lessons are designed to be engaging and age-appropriate, with a focus on making learning fun and accessible for younger students.

Can I use an organ or digital keyboard?

Yes, our lessons are suitable for piano, organ, and digital keyboards. We provide specific guidance for each instrument type, highlighting the unique techniques and approaches required for each.

Is there a trial period?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial so you can experience our teaching method and content before committing to a subscription. No credit card is required for the trial period.

Do you receive personal feedback?

Absolutely! Personal feedback is a cornerstone of our program. You can submit recordings of your playing, and our professional teachers will provide detailed, constructive feedback to help you improve.
